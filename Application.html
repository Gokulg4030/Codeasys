How we integrate IoT with mobile and web apps

We work closely with you at all times. We can connect your website or mobile app to an IoT solution, build a hardware prototype from open hardware components and integrate it into your website or mobile app, or integrate your existing device into a brand new mobile app. As our customer, you can watch your IoT application or platform grow. From idea to prototype to a smart custom IoT platform.

1. Idea

1.Idea This is the first step towards your IoT solution or platform. It's usually a "thing" that you come to us with or we define it together. Here questions like why, how and what are asked. We also help you explore your ideas using Lean Canvas and Value Proposition Canvas.

2. Scheme

We create and define the architecture of the solution as well as the circuit diagram and function of a prototype or already existing IoT device.

3. IoT prototype

We build a device prototype based on a circuit diagram or set of features. Or we can connect existing hardware with a cloud platform or a completely new mobile app.

4. Programming

Once we have a working device prototype or integration into existing hardware, we focus on developing the actual platformdeveloping a web application in the cloud or a mobile application running on iOS or Android operating systems.

5. Robust IoT Platform

Once the actual programming is done, a robust and brilliant IoT platform or solution is created. Ready to welcome the first users. Ready to fulfill your purpose..h6>

6. Support

Its not build , Transfer and Forget .. We also provide user and tech support services to our clients for every IoT app we build for them. you can always be sure that we are nearby to help you with any issue which might arise and that too for as long as you need us.
